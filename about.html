I am a rising senior at Miami University currently studying Computer Science with a minor in Games + Simulation (Game Development). I am most passionate about the field of AI with an interest in Web Development. I find the seemingly limitless potential for AI to solve the world's most complicated problems so captivating and am eager to contribute myself.
