"Factorio, Growth, and its Consequences"

This is a piece of writing in which I begin by drawing parallels between the forces that mandate continuous growth in capitalist society and the mechanics of "Factorio" before showing how the game illuminates several key problems associated with it. This was a project I chose to pursue for my "Rhetoric of Games" course. Shout out to Dr. Phill!
