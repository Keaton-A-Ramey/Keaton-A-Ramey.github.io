Welcome to My Creative Projects!

Includes areas such as writing, game development, 3D Modeling/Animation, and more

"Silent Rapids"

Final Project for "Storyteller as Startup", I wrote and self-published (under a pseudonym) a 150-page novel on Amazon in May 2023. It has sold 20 copies as of writing this.

Narrative Design Documents

Completed in Writing for Games, I wrote two NDDs (one with a team, one solo), describing a storyworld. For one, I created a game and gave a pitch presentation for "funding" from my professor.

"Blogger"

Using GameMaker: Studio 2 with Greyton Manne, we created this comical spin on "Frogger" in which you help a screen-addicted teenager cross the road without dying (at least too much). **Playable Soon

"TimeKeeper"

Using GameMaker: Studio 2 with Greyton Manne, we created this tower defense/top-down shooter in which the player has to fight bots and create towers to fend off waves of enemies across eras. **Playable soon

"The Chief of Uttbredung"

Using Twine, I created a text-based game about a desolate island in the world of Stratostria that faces a difficult moment when, amidst the fighting of their tribes, an advanced, imperical power approaches.

Model + Animation of Young Hall

Using Maya, I created a model with lighting, textures, of Young Hall (where I lived Freshman/Sophomore year). This also included the creation of a 1-minute animation showing off the model.
